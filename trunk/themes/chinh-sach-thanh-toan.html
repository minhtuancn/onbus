Payment policy

You can pay by the following methods:

1. Card Payment

  • International Payments: VISA, MasterCard.
  • Cardholders can pay for themselves or for others.
  • Customers at the station before departure to get tickets 15p. In case the time delay Onbus regulations will ensure that customers have a place to ride there next nearest time.

2. Cash Paymemt

  • Customers pay directly to the employee selling tickets at the departure station.
  • Customers must be at the station 30 minutes before departure to get tickets. OnBus will not be responsible if you do not come before 30p.

General note:

  • The following payments must be made ​​within 24 hours after booking and a minimum of 24 hours prior to departure time, whichever comes first.
  • You should check at the time of acceptance of payment.
  • OnBus not responsible if you do not provide complete and accurate information of customers.
  • In case of necessity OnBus staff will contact you to determine the information about your journey.
  • You can contact the customer support center of Onbus to check the status of your reservation. If necessary you can request tickets sent via email or to the office to pick up tickets OnBus.

Cancellation Policy

On Bus is not responsible for the following cases:

  • Time delay is caused by the bus operator.
  • Bus operator cancellation due to weather issues, politics and other valid reasons.
  • Customers' baggage lost / damaged
  • The car seat changing customer at the last minute to make room for women and children, the elderly, the disabled or the sick.
  • Address passenger provided false or does not exist

When you cancel tickets booked through OnBus Please note that the money is not transferred back to your credit / debit card passengers. Instead, the money will be refunded in the form of coupons that can be used in your next transaction. This coupon valid up to 6 months from the date of cancellation.

The following are the cancellation fees:

  • From 24 hours to 3 days before the journey is a 10% cancellation fee.
  • From 3 days to 1 week before the journey is 5% cancellation fee.

Please note that the cancellation fee and cancellation period may differ according to each car.

* The transaction costs or home delivery charges will not be refunded in case of cancellation

In case of confirmation e-mails and messages delayed or unsuccessful due to technical reasons or as a result of e-mail ID / phone incorrectly provided by the user, etc., tickets will be considered a "ticket was placed" as long as the ticket appears on the confirmation page www.onbus.vn

Lawsuits and complaints related to travel must be reported to the support group OnBus within 10 days from the date of your travel.
