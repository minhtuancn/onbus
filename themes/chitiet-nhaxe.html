Atlantis - The Palm, Dubai

Atlantis The Palm is one of the world’s most iconic resorts. Located in Dubai, this breath-taking resort is the perfect location for the perfect holiday. No matter whether you are looking for a romantic getaway or a fun filled holiday for the whole family, Atlantis The Palm is a modern and exciting holiday destination. You are spoiled for choice when it comes to activities and entertainment. As well as being situated on Dubai’s pristine coastline, you can have an adventure at the Aquaventure Water Park and explore the Dolphin Bay habitat, or indulge in some rest and relaxation at luxurious ShuiQi Spa. Atlantis The Palm is a five-star resort that has been reflected in its lavish design, selection of excellent dining outlets, and remarkable facilities. This resort really is the epitome of luxury.
